| Category                  | Stakeholders                          | Engagement Strategy                                                                 |
|---------------------------|---------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| High Power / High Interest| Lecturer / Project Sponsor, DPO, IT Dept | Weekly progress reports, formal approvals, risk logs, compliance checks, sprint reviews |
| High Power / Low Interest | Campus Management, Procurement / Finance | Executive summaries, milestone briefings, concise cost/risk updates, decision-ready one-pagers |
| Low Power / High Interest | Team Members, Campus Security Staff, Maintenance | Daily standups, backlog visibility, usability testing, SOPs, pilot feedback loops |
| Low Power / Low Interest  | Students / Residents                  | Transparency notices, FAQs, periodic updates only when policies change, feedback channel |
